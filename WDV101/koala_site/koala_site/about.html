Help Save the Koalas

There is currently no legislation, anywhere in the country, that can protect Koalas and Koala habitat in Australia. The listing of the koala as “vulnerable” under the Environmental Protection and Biodiversity Conservation Act in 2012 changed nothing.

By the time you read this page the bulldozers may already be working, but it is not too late to take action.

Here's some ways that you can help save the Koala:

You can help by Donating to the Koala Foundation.

  • Join the Koala Army.
  • Speak or write to your local congressmen.
  • Post information on social media.
  • Adopt a Koala.
  • Conduct a fundraiser.

What you can do if you live in a Koala area:

  • Plant Koala food trees specific to your area.
  • Drive cautiously at night to avoid hitting wildlife.
  • Keep pets indoors at night to protect wildlife.
  • Install escape ropes in pools for trapped animals.
