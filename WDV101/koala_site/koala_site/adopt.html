Make a Difference — Adopt a Koala Today!

By adopting a koala, you’re directly supporting habitat conservation, rescue efforts, and research to help ensure their survival.

Your symbolic adoption helps fund protection programs, medical care, and environmental advocacy.

Each adoption includes:

  • Adoption certificate
  • Full-color koala photo
  • Koala profile and habitat map
  • Updates on your adopted koala

When you adopt a Koala, you receive a beautiful personalised adoption certificate with a photo of your Koala, a welcome letter, Koala stickers, and more.
