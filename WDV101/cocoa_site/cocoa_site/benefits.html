Health Benefits & Fun Facts

Health Benefits of Cocoa

Cocoa is rich in antioxidants like flavonoids that support heart health and reduce inflammation. It may help lower blood pressure and improve brain function.

Mood Enhancement

Cocoa stimulates the release of serotonin and endorphins, promoting happiness and relaxation. A little dark chocolate goes a long way for your mood!

Fun Cocoa Facts

  • Cocoa trees live up to 100 years but produce quality beans for about 25 years.
  • Each cocoa pod contains 20-50 beans.
  • West Africa produces 70% of the world's cocoa.
  • The scientific name of cocoa means "food of the gods."
