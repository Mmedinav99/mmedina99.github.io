Founded in 1996, Pets to Go is Springfield’s only no-kill animal shelter. Since its opening, we’ve placed over 18,000 cats and dogs in loving homes. We rescue animals from kill shelters and accept owner surrenders.

With 15 staff members and over 200 volunteers, we provide exceptional care. Many of our animals experience love for the first time here. Our shelter continues to grow and improve thanks to your support!
