Our History

Pets to Go was founded in 1990 with the mission to provide a safe haven for homeless animals in Springfield. We have grown to become one of the largest no-kill shelters in Illinois.

Our Mission

To rescue, shelter, and find loving homes for cats and dogs while educating the community on responsible pet ownership.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated staff and volunteers work tirelessly every day to care for our animals and find them forever homes.
