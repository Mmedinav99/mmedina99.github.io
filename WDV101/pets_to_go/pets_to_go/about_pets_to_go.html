Our History

Founded in 1996 we are Springfield’s only no-kill animal shelter. Since its opening Pets to Go has placed an estimated 18,000 homeless cats and dogs in new households.

Where do these animals come from? 40% are owner surrenders and the other 60% are animals we rescue from shelters that euthanize.

Our Mission

To rescue, shelter, and find loving homes for cats and dogs while educating the community on responsible pet ownership.

Pets to Go works in conjunction with the Animal Rescue League location services to locate the original owners of lost pets as well as finding new homes for abandoned animals.

Pets To Go relies on donations from United Way, private donations, and volunteers to operate this facility.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated staff of 15 (half are part-time). And our 200-plus volunteers are our backbone. Many of the rescued animals who come to Pets to Go get more love and attention in our shelter than they’ve ever known before. Our shelter continues to grow and improve. Drop by and get to know us better!
