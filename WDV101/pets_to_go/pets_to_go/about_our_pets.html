Meet Our Pets

Our shelter is home to a variety of adoptable dogs and cats who are ready for a second chance. Each pet is evaluated and cared for before being placed for adoption.

Volunteer Opportunities

Interested in helping out? We need animal caretakers, dog walkers, cat cuddlers, and event helpers. Contact us to learn more.

Adoption Process

Visit us during shelter hours to meet our pets. After an interview and home visit, approved adopters can welcome a new furry family member!
